About MediSearch

MediSearch is an AI-driven platform designed to provide reliable and comprehensive information about medicines. We aim to empower individuals with the knowledge to make informed healthcare decisions.

Our platform integrates advanced features like OCR-based prescription scanning, multilingual support, and voice interaction to enhance accessibility and usability. Your health and data security are our priorities.
